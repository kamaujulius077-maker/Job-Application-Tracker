About the Application

CareerPulse is a high-perfomance job tracking utiliy built to help graduates manage their professional pipeline. With a focus on simplicity and efficiency, CareerPulse allows users to easily log job applications, track their progress, and visualize their success rate. Whether you're actively applying or just want to keep an eye on your career journey, CareerPulse is designed to be your go-to companion for staying organized and motivated in the job search process.

Core Features

  • Real-time Data Entry
  • Persistent Local Storage
  • Dynamic Progress Tracking
  • Responsive Grid UI

Development Stack

  • HTML5:Semantic structure
  • CSS3:Custom variables & Flexbox
  • Js:DOM Manipulation
